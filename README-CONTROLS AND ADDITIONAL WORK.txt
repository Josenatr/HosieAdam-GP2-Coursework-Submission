-----------------
CONTROLS
-----------------
W - UP
A - LEFT
S - DOWN
D - RIGHT
SPACE - FORWARD
LALT - BACKWARD

1 - Spawn Monkey
2 - Spawn Wolf
3 - Spawn Rat
4 - Spawn Cat
5 - Spawn Deer
6 - Spawn Cow
7 - Spawn Goat

If the player flies into the model, the model will disappear until they fly away from it.
